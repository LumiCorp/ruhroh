Build a dependency-free grocery budget planner.

Use the provided CSV in `assets/grocery-items.csv` as starter data. The app should let a household:
- review grocery categories and item costs;
- add, edit, and remove items;
- see category totals and a full budget total;
- mark items as planned, purchased, or skipped;
- persist changes locally across page refreshes.

Keep it runnable from the generated workspace without relying on live services.
